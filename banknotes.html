BANK NOTES

The joint oceanic nations have developed new banknotes that celebrate their heritage and indicate their aspirations for the future.

View

BRIEF

The themes of banknotes are culture, history and heritage, and also reflect the daily life of people in 2146.

AR Application

By rotating the pointer, the user can view and understand the history of the relevant area through AR technology.

Design Elements

The main body on the front of the two banknotes is the Sphinx and the statue of Abu Simbel Temple. Because they are underwater, they wear diving helmets and masks, surrounded by corals and fish.

Anti-counterfeiting

The silver dots around the clock can be scanned and a corresponding window will pop up to inform whether the banknote is genuine.
