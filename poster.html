MOTION POSTER

Prominent motion posters are deisgned to mobilise support for subsea settlement and advertise tourism and heritage sites.

BRIEF

The theme of the poster is support for subsea settlement. It shows the changing process of the whole world.

Design Elements

On the iceberg is a polar bear about to fall, and below them is a Nile crocodile with an open mouth. The pyramid is upside down, it represents the current world. Living under the sea is a kind of subversion.

Anti-counterfeiting

The background of the station poster uses the same polka dot elements as the CMIST card to to tie the motion poster in with it.
