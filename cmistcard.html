C-MIST CARD

The Complex-Maglev International Subsea Transport network (C-MIST) requirespassengers to carry swipe cards (C-MIST Cards) giving access to services.

View

BRIEF

The entire C-MIST card is in the shape of a locomotive and has a transparent screen to display route and arrival reminders.

Flexible Screen

A flexible screen is embedded on the reverse side of the card to display route and arrival reminders. The screen part of the card is transparent and will light up to remind the user when necessary.

Design Elements

Regarding the way of carrying, I hope this card is portable and can be carried in a variety of ways.

Portable Card

The silver dots around the clock can be scanned and a corresponding window will pop up to inform whether the banknote is genuine.
